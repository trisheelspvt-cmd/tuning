SIEMENS - UNIFIED SALES

Connecting 18,000+ Sales Representatives Worldwide

Siemens managed a massive global sales team of over 18,000 people, but leads were scattered and response times were slow — often taking days.

🔄 The Turning Point

They implemented Salesforce CRM as a single unified platform for the entire sales organization.

⚙️ What Changed?

  • All customer data centralized in one system
  • Leads automatically routed to the right representative
  • Real-time visibility across global teams

🎯 The Result

  • Response time reduced from days to minutes
  • 100% lead follow-up with AI assistance
  • Significant increase in conversion and revenue
👉 Moral of the story: A unified CRM turns global chaos into seamless collaboration and faster wins.

Acer Inc. – The Delayed Laptop

From Frustration to Instant Support

When Ravi’s new Acer laptop screen failed, he had no visibility — no updates on status, location, or repair timeline. Days turned into silence.

🔄 The Turning Point

Acer introduced a centralized complaint and service management system with real-time tracking.

⚙️ What Changed?

  • Instant ticket generation with unique ID
  • Automatic routing to nearest service center
  • Real-time SMS/email updates for customers

🎯 The Result

  • Customers can track their repair status anytime
  • Repair time reduced significantly
  • Higher customer trust and satisfaction
👉 Moral of the story: Great service isn’t just fixing the product — it’s keeping the customer informed and confident.

Key Components of CRM

1. Operational CRM

  • Handles daily customer interactions with precision.
  • Includes streamlined sales automation and marketing workflows.
  • Integrates service processes to ensure consistent delivery.

2. Analytical CRM

  • Deeply analyzes customer data to uncover hidden patterns.
  • Helps understand complex behaviors and future trends.
  • Provides the intelligence needed for data-driven decision making.

3. Collaborative CRM

  • Shares vital customer info seamlessly across departments.
  • Ensures smooth, jargon-free communication between teams.
  • Breaks down silos to create a unified customer experience.
