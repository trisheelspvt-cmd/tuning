SIEMENS - UNIFIED SALES

Connecting 18,000 Representatives Worldwide

Imagine managing a sales team of over 18,000 people across the world. That was the reality for Siemens.

Leads were coming in from everywhere—but no one knew who was handling what. Some customers waited days for a reply. Others were ignored completely.

It was messy.

🔄 The Turning Point

Siemens decided to fix it by implementing Salesforce as their central CRM system. Suddenly, everything changed.

⚙️ What Changed?

  • All sales data was consolidated in one place
  • Leads were automatically assigned seamlessly
  • Teams were empowered to see exactly what happened in real time

🎯 The Result

What followed their integration?

  • Wait times dropped drastically from days to mere minutes
  • Teams worked together instead of competing blindly
👉 Moral of the story: When everyone works from the same system, speed and clarity improve instantly.

Acer Inc. – The Delayed Laptop

A Journey from Frustration to Instant Support

Ravi had just bought a new Acer laptop for his college work. Everything was smooth—until one day, the screen suddenly stopped working.

Frustrated, he went to the service center. They told him, “We’ll check and get back to you.”

Days passed. No update. Ravi had no idea:

  • Where his laptop was
  • What the problem was
  • When it would be fixed

🔄 The Turning Point

Acer realized many customers were facing the same issue—lack of communication and delays.

So, they introduced a central complaint management system.

⚙️ What Changed?

Now when Ravi raises a complaint:

  • He gets a ticket ID instantly
  • His request is sent to the nearest service center
  • He receives real-time updates via SMS/email
  • Technicians use diagnostic tools to fix issues faster

🎯 The Result

Next time Ravi had an issue:

  • He could track everything
  • His laptop was fixed faster
  • He felt more confident in the brand
👉 Moral of the story: Acer didn’t just fix laptops—they fixed customer trust.

Objectives of CRM

Build Strong Relationships

Move beyond transactions to genuine partnerships. By understanding every touchpoint, we create bonds that stand the test of time, ensuring customers feel seen, heard, and valued at every stage of their journey with us.

Improve Satisfaction

Deliver frictionless experiences that delight. Proactive problem-solving and rapid response times transform standard service into exceptional support, keeping your brand at the top of their mind for all the right reasons.

Increase Retention

Keep your best customers coming back. It's five times more expensive to acquire a new customer than to keep an existing one; CRM gives you the tools to loyalty-proof your business through personalized engagement.

Boost Profitability

Convert data into dollars. Targeted marketing and efficient sales pipelines ensure that resources are invested where they matter most, maximizing lifetime value and driving sustainable revenue growth across the board.

Make every interaction count. In a world of digital noise, hyper-personalization is the ultimate differentiator. Tailor your message, your offers, and your support to the unique profile of every individual user.

Key Components of CRM

1. Operational CRM

  • Handles daily customer interactions with precision.
  • Includes streamlined sales automation and marketing workflows.
  • Integrates service processes to ensure consistent delivery.

2. Analytical CRM

  • Deeply analyzes customer data to uncover hidden patterns.
  • Helps understand complex behaviors and future trends.
  • Provides the intelligence needed for data-driven decision making.

3. Collaborative CRM

  • Shares vital customer info seamlessly across departments.
  • Ensures smooth, jargon-free communication between teams.
  • Breaks down silos to create a unified customer experience.
